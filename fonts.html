01 · Display

Rethena

The brand voice

Reserved for the moments that carry emotion: hero headlines, section titles and big statement numbers. It appears rarely which is exactly why it lands when it does.

  • Use forH1, H2, statement numbers
  • Hero size40–72px (min 28px anywhere)
  • Weight400 — let the size do the talking
Never for paragraphs, buttons, or navigation — it's a display serif and falls apart small.

02 · Accent

Cormorant Garamond

The elegance layer

Always italic, always sparing. It carries the poetic moments taglines under headings, pull-quotes, doctor quotes, and treatment philosophy lines.

  • Use forTaglines, pull-quotes, philosophy lines
  • Size20–32px
  • StyleAlways italic, weight 500–600
This is the seasoning. One or two touches per page max any more and it loses its impact.

03 · Workhorse

Jost

Everything else

The quiet professional. Body copy, navigation, buttons, forms, prices, captions anywhere text simply needs to be read, Jost does the job without asking for attention.

  • Body16–18px · weight 400 · line-height 1.7
  • Nav & buttons14–16px · weight 500 · slight letter-spacing
  • H3 / H420–24px · weight 600
  • AlsoForm labels, footer, prices, captions
Jost carries roughly 85% of the site.
